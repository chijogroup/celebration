Safety note to our beloved friends

  • Drive with caution along the beach road to The Kasturi.
  • Slow down when entering the kampung area towards the venue.
  • If you consume alcohol and cannot drive back to your hotel, inform us, and we will arrange a safe ride for you.
  • Remember to enjoy the laidback beach life!
